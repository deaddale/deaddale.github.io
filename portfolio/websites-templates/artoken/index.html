Who we are?
ARToken will introduce a global ecosystem of AR/VR services through the establishment of a new cooperative model. In this model users and developers benefit from easy 3D object creation and delivery while businesses get access to a broad range of 3D content.
The first industry we brought an impact to is e-commerce. Before us, 3D image creation was time-consuming, complicated and not applicable to large catalogs. Now our software is widely used among online retailers and major e-commerce platforms. Our next step is to provide an impulse forward for the industries standing on the edge of an AR/VR breakthrough.
How do we achieve our vision?
Augmented and virtual realities (AR/VR) have the potential to become a disruptive innovation that will change the business landscape across many industries. However, the lack of 3D content is a constraining factor that prevents the AR/VR’s massive expansion. So, the next wave of the AR/VR revolution will be driven by the content creators working together and contributing to the new ecosystem.
We’ll address the creative content demand with our AR Token ecosystem that makes 3D modelling of real objects open to everyone. The AR/VR tool kits available via the ecosystem platform will allow 3D models to be built with any camera and to be submitted to the 3D content base. The content base will be accessible for endless business cases in the e-commerce, video games and entertainment industries.
What’s in the secret sauce?
ARToken is the first platform that leverages blockchain infrastructure to create, rent and sell 3D content. This approach ensures decentralized and trustless copyrights’ storage and exchange of creative content. Based on smart-contract technology and the novel blockchain architecture EOS (https://eos.io/) which scales to millions of transactions per second, our decentralized platform is designed to empower users to protect and transfer their copyrights.
Ecosystem participants will pay content makers for the right to use or rent their 3D models. The 3D content marketplace and its mobile app will encourage 3D artists to create, rent and sell their products or to use them for personal purposes like demonstrating a 3D models portfolio or integrating them to AR/VR applications. Furthermore, the platform will provide with API and SDK to implement our technologies and community-developed tools as services and applications. In order to support developers with promising AR/VR software solutions and active community members, we will establish The AR/VR Innovation Fund and The Bounty Fund for Contributor

How to get AR Token?
AR Tokens (ART) will be issued to create a decentralized billing system secured by well-designed smart-contracts and will be able to handle a large number of microtransactions. ARTs are going to be used to finance AR/VR ecosystem development and to facilitate 3D content exchange with a single digital currency within the ecosystem community. Thanks to the innovative EOS blockchain, users will not have to pay transaction fees as they would do when using Ethereum or Bitcoin. ART value will depend purely on the AR/VR network ecosystem growth, to be stipulated by the massive expansion of 3D content use and ARToken’s decentralized platform improvements.
Ecosystem members will be able
to pay with ARTs for:
  • Subscription to the 3D content base
  • 3D content purchase at a seller’s price
  • 3D content rent for commercial purpose
Active contributors will be rewarded
with ARTs by:
  • Uploading unique 3D content
  • Verification and moderation of the content
  • Sale or rent out self-created 3D content
